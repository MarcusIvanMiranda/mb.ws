About Us

At Minute Burger, we believe that everyone deserves a delicious burger. That's why we've been working hard since 1982 to create the perfect burger recipe. Our burgers are made with only the freshest ingredients and cooked to perfection every time. We're passionate about burgers and we're passionate about our customers. We believe that everyone should be able to enjoy a great burger at an affordable price. That's why we keep our prices low and our quality high. We're also committed to giving back to the community. We donate a portion of our profits to local charities and organizations that help make the world a better place. We're proud of what we've accomplished over the years, but we're always looking for ways to improve. We're constantly experimenting with new recipes and new ideas to make our burgers even better. Thank you for choosing Minute Burger. We hope you enjoy our burgers as much as we do!
